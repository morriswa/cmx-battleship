How to play: Rules and Scoring

Game background:

Battleship is a two-player strategy game played on ruled grids, where each player secretly places their fleet of warships on a square grid.

The players then take turns calling out coordinates to shoot at the other player's ships, trying to guess their location. The defender responds with "Hit!" or "Miss!"

If a ship is hit, the player records it by placing a red peg in the corresponding hole on their target grid. The opponent then places a red peg in the corresponding hole on their ocean grid for the ship that was hit.

Once all the holes in a ship are filled with red pegs, the ship is considered sunk, and the owner announces it

What you need to play:

  • 2 players
  • Time Limit: 30 minutes
  • Each players must have the same number of ships

Game Objectives:
  • The goal of the game Battleship is to sink all of your opponent's ships before they sink yours.
